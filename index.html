ABOUT US

INSTiGEN was born out of a simple frustration — spending hours manually copying and pasting data from Excel into Microsoft Word just to print inventory stickers. We knew there had to be a faster, smarter, and more consistent way to do it.

That's why we created INSTiGEN — short for Inventory Sticker Generator. Our goal is to make inventory management easier for everyone by automating the process of generating printable stickers straight from your Excel data.
